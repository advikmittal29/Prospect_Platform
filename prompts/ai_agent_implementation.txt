   ```json
       }
   ```

9. PUT /api/agents/{agentId}/policy
   Request:
   ```json

     "max_api_calls_per_run": 100,
     "monthly_budget_limit": 5000,
     "min_confidence_threshold": 0.75,
     "rate_limiting_rpm": 30,
     "concurrent_tasks": 5,
   ```

   Response: Updated policy
